# Pure Blue Fish - Design Guidelines

## Concept
High-tech aquaculture facility, NOT agricultural/farm aesthetic.
Clean, modern, professional appearance. Premium, futuristic feel.

## Color Palette
| Color | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| Pure Blue | #0066CC | Tanks, accents |
| Turquoise | #008B8B | Water, atmosphere |
| White | #F5F5F5 | Floors, walls |
| Gray | #4A4A4A | Steel, frames |
| Green | #228B22 | Plants |

## Required Elements
- Light gray epoxy floor with blue directional lines
- Digital monitoring screens on walls
- Workers in white lab coats (when showing people)
- Plants along walkways
- Blue LED lighting on tank edges
- Clear blue water with visible fish
- Stainless steel railings and catwalks

## MUST AVOID
- Farm/agricultural aesthetic (hay, dirt, rust)
- Murky or green water
- Messy exposed pipes and wiring
- Dark, industrial atmosphere
- Worn or weathered appearance
